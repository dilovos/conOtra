Synopsis

After being missing for almost 20 years, Rick Sánchez shows up unexpectedly at his daughter Beth's house. He goes to live with her and her family using the garage as his personal laboratory.

BUT ¿WHO IS RICK SANCHEZ REALLY?

He is an alcoholic scientist who kidnaps his impressionable grandson, Morty, to live dangerous adventures through our cosmos and parallel universes.
